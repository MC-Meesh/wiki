# Job Search Plan

Job search strategy from CMU era (Fall 2024).

## Daily Routine

### Networking (30m)

Shooting for outreach to 3-5 alum/individuals of interest

### Technical Practice (1h)

1-2 [Neetcode 150](https://neetcode.io/roadmap) problems w/ review

### Applications (15m)

1-2 apps per day

### Behavioral Practice (15m)

Practice a sample behavioral with GPT

## Technical

[Neetcode 150](https://neetcode.io/roadmap)

## Networking

### Sample Email

> Hi [Name],
>
> I hope you're doing well. I'm a CMU MS AI student, and I'm intrigued by your role at [Company].
>
> If possible, I'd appreciate your insights as I tailor my research and prepare for the job market:
>
> What problems do you tackle at [Position] in [Company]?
>
> How can I shape my research to stand out for similar positions?
>
> Would you have 15 minutes this week for a quick call to discuss your experience?
>
> Thank you in advance,
