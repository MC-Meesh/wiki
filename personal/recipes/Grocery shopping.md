    Breakfast Burritos (6 burritos, ~35-40g protein each)

    TJ list:
    - Eggs (18-count)
    - Liquid egg whites (carton, for extra protein without more fat)
    - Chicken chorizo or turkey sausage
    - Shredded cheese (sharp cheddar or pepper jack)
    - Hash browns (frozen shredded) or their potato bites
    - Large burrito-size flour tortillas
    - Salsa verde or their Hatch salsa
    - Avocado (buy day-of to add fresh)

    Prep: Brown sausage, set aside. Crisp hash browns in the fat. Scramble 8 eggs + 1 cup egg whites soft.
     Combine everything + cheese. Fill tortillas, wrap tight in foil, freeze 5 / fridge 1. Reheat: 90s
    microwave from fridge, or oven 375°F 25 min from frozen.

    Protein boosters worth grabbing

    - Cottage cheese (snack or blend into scramble)
    - Greek yogurt tubs
    - Pre-cooked lentils (side)
